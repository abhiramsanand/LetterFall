INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Catch as many falling letters as you can within 15 seconds using the pot provided.
  2. You have 3 minutes to form as many words as possible from the caught letters.
  3. Points are calculated as 2 times the number of letters in the word. Longer words earn more points!
  4. Click the "PLAY" button to begin the game and test your word-catching skills!
  5. Use the shuffle option wisely to uncover hidden word combinations.
